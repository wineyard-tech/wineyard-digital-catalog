# WineYard Digital Catalog — Week 1 Performance Report
**Period:** 13–19 April 2026 · **Prepared:** 20 April 2026

---

## Executive Summary

The catalog went live on 13 April 2026 with 119 integrators enabled across 4 outlets. In 7 days, **29 active integrators** generated **67 estimates worth ₹11.0L**, converting to **30 invoices worth ₹4.14L** — with **zero additional staff effort** from WineYard.

At current run rates and with 119 users enabled, the catalog is already on track for **₹17.7L/month** in invoice revenue — exceeding the ₹10L/month target.

**87% of invoices by count came from the app.** The 30% value gap is driven by one large ₹1.1L Zoho-direct order (MANIKANTA TECHNOLOGIES) — if that had come through the app, catalog share would be 93% by value.

**Key insight:** ₹1.22L of business from these same integrators still went through WineYard staff in Zoho during the same week they were using the catalog. This represents orders that still cost staff time to process. Phase 2's core goal is shifting these to self-serve.

## What Phase 2 unlocks - Next opportunities

Phase 2 builds on Week 1 validation with three focused goals, scoped for Q2–Q3 2026.

### Goal 1 — Integrator Success (Engagement & Retention)
Give integrators tools that make the catalog useful beyond just pricing enquiries.
- **Share quote with custom markup** — integrators forward a branded quote to their end customers with their own margin built in
- **Estimate editing** — change items or quantities on a submitted estimate without restarting
- **Did You Know** — daily product/industry fact to build engagement and return visits
- **Festival greetings** — shareable branded creatives for integrators to send to their customer base

### Goal 2 — Distributor Visibility (Strategic Operations)
Replace gut-feel with data. WineYard management sees what's happening across all outlets, daily.
- **Usage dashboard** — estimates, invoices, active users by outlet (daily/weekly/monthly)
- **Missed opportunity alerts** — estimates with no invoice conversion after 3 days, flagged by outlet
- **Product demand trends** — which SKUs are being estimated most; stock signal before it becomes a shortage
- **Customer cohort view** — active vs. dormant integrators by outlet; who to re-engage

### Goal 3 — Scale Readiness (Advanced Capabilities)
Infrastructure to handle 500–1,000+ integrators without proportional staff load.
- **In-app order confirmation** — integrator confirms directly; no WhatsApp reply needed
- **Payment integration** — UPI and credit orders, collected before WineYard staff involvement
- **Auto Sales Order in Zoho** — zero manual entry on confirmed orders
- **Push notifications & reminders** — re-engage browsing integrators; remind on pending estimates
- **Admin dashboard** — WineYard staff enable/disable integrators, view login status, resend access links

At 200+ users, the manual follow-up burden becomes a bottleneck. Phase 2 is the infrastructure to scale without adding staff.
